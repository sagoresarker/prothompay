About Us.

Prothom Pay is an emerging cashless QR code based shopping platform to offer hassle-free transactions through digital money and close the loop between customers, merchants, partners and financial institutions. It enables customers to transact locally and globally through various online and mobile payment services using our mobile app. Also, there is a product review system for customers to analyse market products.

We are a merchant focused commerce platform which provides easy and secure methods to accept payments through capturing QR code from products. The vision is to create a cashless society with a better shopping experience where you never have to worry about safety. We want to ensure a trustworthy system where you never have to compromise on your digital privacy while sharing your data. Shopping will always be a pleasure with Prothom Pay.
